Problem Formulation

Assumptions


  • Env- Several Abstract Cubes
  • Action - Fixed throttle(speed), steering direction (L,L-,F,R-,R), no back, no brake
  • State - Image of front-view
  • Reward - -100 collide, +1 moving away, +100 surpass
  • Restart after collide
  • Predict > long-range sense & real-time action
